{% block title %}Overview (part 1/5){% endblock %}

The study session is divided into five parts

  1. An overview of the study. (1-2 min)
  2. A short demographic survey. (1-2 min)
  3. Task A: Working with a robot to move boxes in a room. (8-10 min)
  4. Task B: Working with a robot to clean a room. (8-10 min)
  5. A short post-experiment questionnaire. (1-2 min)

For each of the two tasks, you will be first given a tutorial to describe the task. Then, you will complete a set of practice sessions followed by the final task. Your goal is to complete these tasks as efficiently as possible in collaboration with the robot.
