{% block title %}Task B: Rescue (part 4/5){% endblock %}

Great! You have completed the first task of the experiment.

Now, we will proceed to the second task. The team includes two members: a police car and a fire truck. In this task, you will control the “police car” and complete the task with the “fire truck”. The task environment, police car, and fire truck are shown below.

In this task, your team's goal is to rescue as many people as possible in 30 time steps. There are three places that need your help: City Hall, Campsite, and Mall. Rescue at City Hall and Campsite can be done by either the police car or fire truck alone. However, to rescue people at the Mall, both the police car and fire truck need to work together to repair one of the bridges (yellow signs near the mall).

During the task, you can control the police car shown below. The fire truck moves autonomously. To learn more about how to complete this task, you will complete

  • A tutorial session.
  • Four test sessions.

Let’s begin!
