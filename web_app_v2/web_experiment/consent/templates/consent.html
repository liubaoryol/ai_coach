{% block title %}Online Study on Teamwork{% endblock %}

This online study is designed to better understand teamwork and develop computational approaches to model and assess teamwork. It is being conducted by Sangwon Seo and Prof. Vaibhav Unhelkar at Rice University.

During this study, you will complete a set of tasks with a robot teammate in a virtual environment (much like a video game) and answer a few questions. Your study session will take up to 25 minutes. You will receive a $5 gift card for successful completion of the study.

For more details, please review the consent form for participation in this study. If you agree to participate, please enter your User ID and click the "I agree" button.

This study is designed to be completed on a computer or a laptop. Please do not participate in the study via a cell phone or tablet.

Please use Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. Safari may not work depending on the version. Internet Explorer is not supported.
